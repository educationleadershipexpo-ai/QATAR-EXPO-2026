Join a 24-Hour Innovation Hackathon

In collaboration with Hi Loop, the Qatar Education Leadership Expo 2026 proudly presents the Vibe Coding Challenge — a 24-hour innovation hackathon designed to inspire the next generation of creative problem solvers. This challenge invites students across Qatar to reimagine the university experience of tomorrow using the power of AI, design thinking, and digital tools — no coding experience required.

Register to Participate

Challenge Theme

“The Future of Higher Education – Student Life Reimagined”

How can we make university life more connected, inclusive, and student-centered through technology?

Participants will design digital solutions (apps, websites, or AI-driven tools) that enhance global student life.

Your goal is to identify a real-world problem students face and design a creative, impactful solution.

Example Concepts:

  • An AI-powered course advisor that matches students with ideal subjects and mentors.
  • A cultural buddy app to help Qatari students adapt to new countries.
  • A financial wellness assistant that helps manage budgeting, part-time work, and scholarships.

Awards & Recognition

Impress the judges and win incredible prizes to kickstart your academic and professional journey.

Prizes for Winners

Win scholarships or tuition discounts sponsored by partner universities, receive Certificates of Excellence, and gain recognition at the Expo.

Judging Criteria

Teams will be judged on Creativity & Innovation, Empathy & Real-world Impact, Global Relevance, and Pitch Presentation.

Unmatched Exposure
